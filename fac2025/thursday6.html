David McCreary

Short and to the point, David is an entertainer. For nearly 25 years, David has been performing comedy and magic at events for some of the biggest companies in the world, including Fed Ex, Honda of America, and Limited Brands, just to name a few. While David loves fooling the Big Wigs at these companies, nothing fulfills him like using his talents to impact students. David speaks to them as young men and women, respecting the fact that they know the difference between right and wrong. David knows students don't need to be talked down to, so he uses an approach that is positive and appealing.
