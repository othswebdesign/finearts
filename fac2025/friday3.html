Paige Afton

Paige Lehr, rebranded and going by her first and middle name: Paige Afton. She grew up in Grand Ridge, IL and learned to play guitar when she was 14 through a friend and watching a lot of YouTube videos. She is a solo acoustic artist and has been performing at a range of local events, bars, restaurants, and weddings since 2017. Her first public performance was at the one and only OHS during Fine Arts - sophomore through senior year. She plays cover songs from the 90s to today's music. Paige also plans to include some original songs!
