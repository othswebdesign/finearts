808 Beezy – Musician, Singer

The eccentric 808 BEEZY is what happens when optimism meets melodic trap. The Arkansas-raised artist wraps emotional storytelling over hard-hitting 808s. From concept to delivery, THE EMERGING artist effortlessly captivates and woos his listeners with witty punchlines and ear-grabbing production.
