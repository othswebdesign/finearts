The Throwbacks -- Band -- The Throwbacks Official Website

The Throwbacks follow one of rock 'n' roll's richest traditions: covering beloved songs that almost everyone knows deep within their psyches. Since 2015, they have reminded audiences of the music that has been popular for 70 years and give it a fresh coat of paint. What if The Beatles wrote 'Twist and Shout' today? What would that sound like? The vision is to keep the spirit of rock and roll oldies alive and provide a criminally underserved era of music some much deserved attention. For nearly a decade, they have entertained thousands across Chicago at small clubs, corporate events, park districts and festivals alike
