Sung Beats

A master of live vocal instrumentation, SungBeats mimics percussion, scratches, basslines, trumpets, strings, and many other sound effects to create organic music with the human voice.

His show consists of vocal remixing of your favorite hit songs, as well as interactive segments where your students will get silly and learn the basics of beatboxing.
