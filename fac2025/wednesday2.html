Brady Lee

Brady Lee's official website, and photo credit.

Milwaukee-born singer, songwriter, and performer Brady Lee is a dynamic artist known for his captivating on-stage presence. Drawing inspiration from artists like Kane Brown, Ed Sheeran, and Dan + Shay, Brady's sound is a mix of pop-infused, radio-friendly anthems that highlight his exceptional vocal talent and charismatic performance style.
