Jason LeVasseur

A Fine Arts Favorite for 20+ years, Jason entertains with his music and interacts with students to deliver positive messages. Beatboxing to dancing, he never disappoints to keep students engaged and thinking.

Jason LeVasseur lives in Nashville, TN. and is the most awarded music performer in the history of campus entertainment.
