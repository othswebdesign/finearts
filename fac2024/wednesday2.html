Bill Myers-Storyteller

In 1975, OTHS English teacher had a crazy idea to create a festival that encompassed and celebrated a variety of performing arts. Throughout the fifty years, the Fine Arts Club and The Fine Arts Festival has adjusted and adapted to the wants and needs of OTHS students and staff, but the intent is still the same. Myers has built upon his love of language as Starved Rock's very own storyteller. His stories focus on life experiences, family, Irish folktales with a dash of wisdom and fire.
