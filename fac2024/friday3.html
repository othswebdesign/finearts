3rd Hour (10:17-11:03) DJ Lee

David Jonathan "DJ" Lee is a beatboxer, songwriter, and performer, who began his career as the lead vocalist in an indie band, recording seven albums and touring the world. He has performed with the likes of The Zac Brown Band & The Roots. His latest project is a real cool three-piece rock band from Nashville, TN called, "The Magi". As a solo artist, DJ performs using his voice and a looping pedal to create layers of vocal percussion and harmonic tones as he performs fun cover songs and heartfelt original songs. He loves to engage with audiences with his "personalized freestyle improvisation" and he is extremely excited to spend the day with all the amazing students from Ottawa High School.
