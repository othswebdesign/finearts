OHS Fine Arts Club Presents…

The Annual Fine Arts Festivial! Where students and preformers can show their skills and arts of their craft to everybody in their school! This year OTHS's Fine Arts Club celebrates 50 years! Keeping with tradition, Fine Arts Club members take a satirical look at our lives here at OTHS and the world itself. Mark Twain said, "Man's greatest gift is his sense of humor." This is echoed by Jimmy Buffet when he sings, "If we couldn't laugh, we would all go insane." this couldn't be anymore true as the laughter and joy from this event is probably what is keeping us sane. Along with that, our other preformances of music and tricks up magicians' sleeves with many more other types of entertainment! come join us as we show off the preformances of many talented individuals from Wednesday, March 20th to Friday, March 22nd as we celebrate 50 years of the Fine Arts Festival!
