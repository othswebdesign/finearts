Angie K



Angie K is a country singer that likes to mix some spanish into her amazing music as a nod to her latin roots. She was born in El Salvador, raised in Georgia, and likes to travel, spreading her country music all around the world and finding a crowd anywhere she goes. She was inspired by the fact that anywhere she went there were people that loved country music and how it was intwined into her childhood.
