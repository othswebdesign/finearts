D'Lisa Flannigan

D'Lisa Flannigan describes herself as a "product of the Chicago school system". While growing up she witnessed how "life's circumstances" affected each person in different ways. D'Lisa accepted her calling as an encourager and motivator and now loves to travel the world giving youth practical information on how to "Rise Above" life's circumstances. Never a dull moment when D'Lisa is on the mic, she will make your kids laugh, and maybe cry. She is an excellent communicator and the main message of "You are capable, and Rise Above your circumstances" will ring out loud and clear.
