Kevi Morse

The Evansville/Indianapolis, Indiana product (now based in Grand Rapids, Michigan) Kevi Morse does over fifty shows a year. He is currently on two tours at once. One of the tours is for RemedyLIVE. The tour is called the “GST Tour.” The tour furthers RemedyLIVE’s mission to betterment of mental health and the stigma that surrounds it. The concurrent tour that Kevi Morse is also on right now is the “Floored Tour.”
Kevi Morse is always looking to inspire, so he created this tour with just that intent and theme. Kevi Morse states, “The ‘Floored Tour’ is meant to inspire others to tap into their purpose.” Kevi Morse continues: “I want to inspire the people to put the pedal to the metal on their dreams and aspirations, floor it, and make it happen. The [Floored] tour has been lit so far!” Just like his message to the fans, Kevi Morse is always on go even on the go.
