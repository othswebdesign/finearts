Dueling Pianos

Midwest Dueling Pianos is a collection of some of the best entertainers in the industry. They perform their unique brand of interactive entertainment all over the country for events such as weddings, college shows, civic events, corporate functions, and more. They offer a wildly entertaining music and comedy dueling piano show for weddings, as well as college, civic, and corporate events throughout Michigan, Ohio, the Midwest, and the country.
