The Student Body-Band

Chicago’s HOTTEST and most engaging cover band is ripped right out of your high school yearbook pages! A fun and entertaining play on all the cliques that ruled your school, The Student Body plays all your favorite high energy party anthems! With their infectious and humorous stage show, soaring three-part harmonies, and medleys that keep you guessing.

Besides playing clubs, casinos, and festivals..The Student Body is also available for reunions, weddings, corporate events, and private parties – and is available to travel nationwide. They can dress formally or in full character for your special event.
