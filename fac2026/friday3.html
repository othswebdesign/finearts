Ryan Wotherspoon

Ryan Wotherspoon is a keynote speaker, actor, singer, composer, producer, writer, and videographer whose work blends heart, craft, and storytelling in unforgettable ways. His love for music, creativity and human connection has led to collaborations with powerhouses like Stephen King, John Mellencamp, T Bone Burnett, Bruce Greenwood, Sally Struthers, Shuler Hensley, Joe Morton, Rick Allison, and Richard Marx.
