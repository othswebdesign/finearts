The Wayouts

The Wayouts are: Steve Cimino – Drums, Vocals Eric Korte – Bass, Vocals Brian McDonnell – Guitar, Vocals Jeff Palac – Guitar, Vocals The Wayouts are the newest standout on the Chicago festival scene. Dedicated exclusively to garage rock hits from the 1960s, the Wayouts deliver an authentic revival of rock n’ roll’s first generation with hits from The Kinks, The Animals, The Troggs, The Monkees, The Zombies, The Sonics and tons more — songs everybody knows with no run-of-the-mill “oldies”. The Wayouts are a family-friendly, dance-crazy, beer-tent shaking revival of rock n’ roll’s first-generation anthems, appealing equally to hipsters, soccer moms and grandparents. The carefully curated Wayouts set list is made up exclusively of songs that everybody knows but aren’t worn out by your average classic rock and oldies acts. There are no computers, no synthesizers and no gimmicks … just 4 guys and the coolest songs ever written. All veterans of the Chicago music scene, The Wayouts’ members have diverse musical resumes including signed, original indie rock projects, solo singer/songwriters and high-profile cover bands. The four friends discovered a shared love of 1960s garage rock anthems and recognized an opportunity to offer something different in Chicago’s live music scene. Formed in 2017, the Wayouts’ broad appeal was immediately recognized by fans and promoters alike. Their meticulous set selection, raucous delivery and expert musicianship quickly landed them premier billing at summer festivals and premier venues throughout the Chicago area including Rosemont’s Parkway Bank Park, Naperville Rib Fest and Wrigley Field’s Gallagher Way.
