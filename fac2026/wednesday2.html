Christina Eltrevoog

Singer-songwriter, Ottawa High graduate and former FAC member, Christina Eltrevoog returns to her alma mater. She has performed throughout the United States.
Emerging from her roots in Midwestern US, Christina engages audiences from the heartland to the coasts with honest songs expressed in rich layers. She approaches music with reverence to moment, regard for the past, and exploration of the future.
