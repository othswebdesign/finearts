Kacey Layne

Kacey Layne is a comedian, actress, and host, who you may have seen on GLEE, ScreenRant, What’s Trending, Wizpop, and more! Her musical comedy has been featured in the parody musicals Game of Thrones: The Musical (Off-Broadway and San Diego Comic-Con), where she played “Daenerys: Mother of Dragons,” and Lost: The Musical as “Claire.”She has written for pop culture comedy shows like Pop Cultured with Jimmy Pardo and TMI Hollywood, and was a finalist for NBC’s Bring the Funny with her sketch group, The Blonde Jokes. Kacey’s original musical comedy has also taken off online—her music page on Instagram has gained over 1 million views, drawing fans with her sharp wit and catchy, comedic takes.
